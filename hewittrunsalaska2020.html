Track Tim as he attempts to traverse Alaska on foot pulling his supply sled Cookie 1,304 miles along the 2020 Irondog Route from Fairbanks Alaska along the frozen rivers and through passes to the coast. From there Tim will turn north and cross the frozen Norton Sound of the Bering Sea well above the arctic circle for a 400 mile loop before returning to the sea and heading west to Nome to finish. The course will be beautiful and challenging.

Tim will start in the heart of winter, two weeks before the snowmobile race to optimize his chances for a trail for the harshest part of the expedition above the arctic circle.
