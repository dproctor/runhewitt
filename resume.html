RUNNING RESUME

40 years of running marathons, ultras and the world's toughest race, the Iditarod Trail Invitational

I started running in the early 1980s after graduating from law school. Having been a gymnast in college, I realized I needed to find a new way to exercise. I started with marathons, then graduated to ultra-marathons, and then I learned about the Iditarod. I am an ultra-runner who loves to test myself: I've completed more than 60 marathons and ultras in my career.

Here are some of my more noteworthy finishes.
